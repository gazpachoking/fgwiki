## Specify group (eg. anime fansubs)
**Example:**


    series:
      - fullmetal alchemist brotherhood:
          from_group: eclipse
      - naruto:
          from_group:
            - horriblesubs
            - crunchysubs

**Supported notations:**

* [Group] Series
* Series XviD-Group

To disallow certain groups use [[regexp|Plugins/regexp]] plugin to reject them.
